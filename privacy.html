Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CupFut ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and authentication data when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Avatar, friend list, match history, and game statistics.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and push notification tokens.
  • Usage Data: Game interactions, feature usage, and performance data to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app experience.
  • Send push notifications about game invites and updates (with your consent).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Display personalized ads through third-party ad networks.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication.
  • Firebase: Push notifications and analytics.
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertising.
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at cupfut.app@gmail.com.
